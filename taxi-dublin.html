📋 Basic Requirements

To become a licensed taxi driver in Dublin, you will generally need to:

✔ Hold a valid driving licence
✔ Meet the National Transport Authority (NTA) requirements
✔ Pass the SPSV Driver Entry Test
✔ Complete Garda Vetting
✔ Meet the required medical standards
✔ Apply for your SPSV Driver Licence
