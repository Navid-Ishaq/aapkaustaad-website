Preparation for the Official VTC Examination in the Community of Madrid

The Official VTC Examination is an important requirement for individuals who wish to pursue a professional career in the passenger transport sector within the Community of Madrid.

At AL-NOOR Centre Educatiu Asiàtic, we provide structured educational support to help students understand the examination content through clear explanations, guided learning, and practical preparation designed to build knowledge and confidence.

What You Will Study

The preparation programme covers a wide range of topics, including:

  • Spanish language skills required for the examination.
  • Map reading and route interpretation.
  • Knowledge of important streets and locations throughout Madrid.
  • Professional customer service.
  • Passenger assistance and accessibility.
  • Basic first aid.
  • VTC regulations and legal requirements.
  • Practice with examination-style questions and realistic mock tests.

Our Educational Approach

At AL-NOOR Centre Educatiu Asiàtic, we provide a supportive learning environment that enables students to prepare for the examination in a clear and structured way.

Our preparation includes:

  • Easy-to-understand explanations.
  • Personalised educational support.
  • Online classes.
  • Practice with examination-style questions.
  • Assistance in Urdu, Hindi, Punjabi, and Spanish.
  • Continuous guidance throughout the learning process.

Professional Opportunities

Obtaining the Official VTC qualification can open the door to new professional opportunities within Madrid's passenger transport sector.

Some of the key advantages include:

  • A modern and expanding industry.
  • Ongoing demand for qualified professional drivers.
  • Opportunities for stable employment.
  • Greater flexibility in working arrangements.
  • Long-term professional development.

Our Commitment

At AL-NOOR Centre Educatiu Asiàtic, we are committed to providing responsible, accessible, and high-quality educational preparation, helping every student approach the Official VTC Examination with confidence and a solid foundation of knowledge.
